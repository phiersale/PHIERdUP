The Betrayal — By the Numbers

This week alone, the VA tried to quietly slip a rule into the Federal Register — effective immediately, no public notice — that would have cut disability benefits for veterans who take medication. A veteran with 100% PTSD rating could have dropped to 30% simply because their medication was working.

They backed off after 10,000+ comments in 60 hours and a national outcry. But the rule is still on the books. They can reinstate it any time.
