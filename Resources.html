❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers. No jargon.
Is this realistic?

Yes — because it's based on math, not hope. Telehealth is already cheaper and faster. Universal healthcare already saves $2.73T. UBI is funded by redirecting waste, not raising taxes.

Why telehealth first?

Because it's the first domino. Telehealth is 10x cheaper, instantly scalable, already working, already nationwide. Once telehealth is authorized, the entire 5D cascade becomes possible.

Why 3.5%?

Harvard research (Erica Chenoweth) shows that when 3.5% of a population organizes, systemic change becomes guaranteed, peaceful, and inevitable. For the U.S., that's 11.6 million people.

Is this partisan?

No. Healthcare, stability, jobs, and democracy are not partisan issues. They're human issues. This movement includes conservatives, liberals, independents, and apolitical Americans.

How is this funded?

Through the $2.73 trillion we're already wasting. No new taxes. No new agencies. Just redirecting money from insurance profits to universal coverage.

What if Congress still refuses?

Then we replace them — peacefully, legally, and at scale. Primaries, general elections, district-level mobilization. No violence. Just organized civic power.
