🛡️ Demand #5: Guardrails on Authoritarianism

Use economic leverage to prevent executive overreach

What We Demand

Congressional guardrails on executive power, enforced by the economic leverage of 234 million organized Americans.

How This Works

Once PHIERS delivers universal healthcare + UBI + jobs, we prove we don't need executive overreach.

We use that proof as leverage to force Congressional accountability measures on any president — current or future.

Specific Guardrails

  • Checks on executive orders that bypass Congressional authority
  • Restoration of Congressional war powers
  • Protection of civil service from political purges
  • Limits on emergency declarations

Why This Is Dimension #5

Healthcare, jobs, and economic security give us the leverage to demand political accountability. Without those first, we have no power. With them, we have unstoppable power.
