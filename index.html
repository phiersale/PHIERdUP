What Is PHIERS?

Politicians respond to organized power.
PHIERS organizes the public.

PHIERS isn't a policy campaign. It's a way for regular Americans to give Congress documented direction — and replace the ones who ignore it.

We started with nothing but a truth too heavy to carry alone — and a belief that millions of Americans were feeling it too. What began as one spark has become a shared recognition, a shared frustration, and now a shared movement. We're building something grounded, disciplined, and impossible to ignore. And if you're here, you're already part of it.

When millions of people say the same thing — on the record — Congress has to act. We don't ask people to yell louder. We help them line up behind one clear demand.

We use math and collective power to force Congress to stabilize healthcare, protect coverage, and stop using people's lives as bargaining chips.

America is getting squeezed from all sides — rising costs, unstable jobs, political gridlock, and falling trust. These problems are connected. They need a connected solution.

Healthcare is just the start. PHIERS is built as a permanent coordination system — a way for Americans to organize around practical solutions across the economy, national security, and more. When one part stabilizes, the others get stronger.
