Consumer Power

Make the Market Work For You

When millions shop as individuals, corporations set the terms. When millions shop as a group, we set the terms.

12 million people shopping together is enough to move markets and change workplaces.

Here's what that actually looks like: We buy from the same suppliers Target uses. The same manufacturers. The same distributors. Except we cut out every middleman. No markups. No profit extracted from people who can't afford it. No CEO bonus built on the back of your grocery bill.

We pass every penny of savings directly to our members.

That's not a boycott. That's outcompeting them on their own turf — with their own suppliers — at prices they can't match because they need to keep extracting profit from you to survive.

When a company disrespects its customers, exploits its workers, or treats people like a resource to drain — we don't just walk away. We replace them. We build the people's marketplace right next to them and we undercut them permanently.

No profit motive. No middlemen. No markups. Just people buying as a team for the benefit of everyone on the team.

This is PHIERSale. It started with medications sixteen years ago — proving that buying direct from manufacturers could cut drug costs by 80% without a single law being passed, without asking anyone's permission. Now it scales to everything. Groceries. Essentials. Everything corporations have been overcharging you for while disrespecting you at the same time.

They underestimated us. That's about to be expensive for them.
