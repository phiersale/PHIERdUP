What Is PHIERS?

PHIERS is a national citizen coalition built on one simple idea: when millions of Americans document the same demand, Congress has no choice but to act. We don't ask people to shout louder — we align them around the same, documented direction.

We use math, not ideology — and leverage, not hope — to force Congress to stabilize healthcare, protect coverage, and stop using people's lives as bargaining chips.

America is facing simultaneous pressures — rising costs, unstable jobs, political gridlock, and declining trust. These problems are connected. They require a connected solution.

But healthcare is just the start. PHIERS is designed as a permanent coordination system — a framework that allows Americans to organize around practical solutions across the economy, national security, and beyond, creating a cohesive tapestry where solutions in one area reinforce stability in others.

The goal is simple: make America better and healthier, in ways that last longer than any election cycle.
