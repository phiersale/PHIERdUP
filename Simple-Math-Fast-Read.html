The PHIERS Solution

PHIERS (People's Health Insurance Endowment and Referral Service) converts waste in the insurance system into coverage people actually use.

By replacing expensive insurance with $600/year telehealth-first coverage, government savings fund:

  • Universal healthcare access
  • Millions of healthcare jobs
  • Massive system-wide savings

This uses existing law and proven models - no new taxes.

Why Medicaid Comes First

Reform usually fails because it tries to cover everyone at once. PHIERS starts where savings are largest and people are most vulnerable.

  • Highest cost per person = highest savings
  • Protects low-income, elderly, and disabled first
  • Creates proof at massive scale

Once Medicaid works, expansion to ACA, private insurance, and the uninsured becomes inevitable.
